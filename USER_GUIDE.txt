===========================================
   LinkedIn Profile Scraper - User Guide
===========================================

WHAT THIS DOES
--------------
Walks through a LinkedIn People-search result list and saves all the
profile URLs to a CSV file you can open in Excel.


REQUIREMENT
-----------
You need Google Chrome installed on your PC.
If you don't have it: https://www.google.com/chrome/


HOW TO RUN
----------
1. Double-click LinkedInScraper.exe

2. A black console window appears showing the app's web address.

3. Your default browser opens automatically to the app.
   If it doesn't, copy the URL shown in the console (something like
   http://127.0.0.1:5000) and paste it into your browser.

4. SIGN IN:
   - In the top card, enter your LinkedIn email and password
   - Click Save
   - These are stored locally on your PC only - never sent anywhere
     except linkedin.com itself.

5. BUILD A SEARCH ON LINKEDIN:
   - Go to linkedin.com in another tab
   - Click the search bar -> People
   - Click "All filters" on the right
   - Tick whatever you want: location, industry, company, seniority,
     school, verifications, etc.
   - Click "Show results"
   - COPY THE URL from your browser's address bar

6. PASTE THE URL into the app's "LinkedIn People-Search URL" field

7. (Optional) Tick "Only verified accounts" to keep only profiles with
   LinkedIn's blue checkmark

8. Click "Start Scraping"

9. A new Chrome browser window opens (your own installed Chrome).
   The app logs into LinkedIn in that window.
   - If LinkedIn asks for a captcha or verification code: SOLVE IT
     in that Chrome window. The app waits up to 5 minutes.

10. Wait for it to finish. The progress bar shows page X of Y.

11. Click "Download CSV" when it's done.


THINGS TO KNOW
--------------
* Scrapes can take 20-40 minutes for a full 100-page result list.
  This is intentional - going faster gets your LinkedIn account flagged.

* LinkedIn limits free accounts to a small number of people-searches
  per month. If the scrape stops at 6-8 results, you've hit that cap.
  Wait until next month, or use a LinkedIn Premium account.

* DO NOT close the black console window while scraping. Closing it
  stops the app.

* All your data (saved CSVs, your login, the Chromium browser) is
  stored at:
     C:\Users\YOURNAME\AppData\Local\LinkedInScraper\


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
"Windows protected your PC" on first launch:
  Click "More info" -> "Run anyway". This appears because the .exe
  is not code-signed (would cost $200/year). It's safe.

Browser doesn't open automatically:
  Copy the URL from the console window into your browser manually.

"Address already in use":
  Close any other copy of this app that's already running.

Scrape returns 0 results:
  - Make sure the URL is a "People" search URL, not Jobs/Posts/etc.
  - Try a less restrictive filter to confirm the app is working.

Login fails:
  - Double-check email and password in the app's top card.
  - LinkedIn sometimes asks for a verification code by SMS or email.
    Watch the Chromium window and complete the challenge there.
